Marketing

Our Marketing subteam organizes team events, manages the budget, maintains alumni and corporate relationships, and secures funding for the team. Members manage the team's image by coding improvements for our website and developing creative designs for merchandise and promotional material. This subteam is also responsible for displaying CRC's progress and personality through social media. The Marketing subteam consists of two subsystems: Marketing. However, most of the subteam's work is structured around the projects that occur throughout the school year, whose work often requires a mix of the two subsystems.
